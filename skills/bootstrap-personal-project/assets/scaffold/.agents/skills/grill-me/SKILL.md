name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when the user wants to stress-test a plan, pressure-test a design, get grilled on their approach, find the holes/gaps or hidden assumptions in their thinking, or be challenged with hard questions before building — including phrasings like "grill me", "grilleame", "cuestioná mi plan", "desafiá mis supuestos", "hacéme las preguntas difíciles", "buscá los huecos de mi diseño", or "poné a prueba esta idea".
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
